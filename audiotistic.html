AUDIOTISTIC

  • Location: Mountain View, CA
  •  
  • Date: July
  •  
  • Duration: 2 Days
  •  
  • Time of Day: Day + Night
  •  
  • Stages: 2
  •  
  • Genre: Various
  •  
  • Host: Insomniac
PRODUCTION

Audiotistic is really a local festival, and thus, is not of the same scale as many of the other festivals that Insomniac puts on. With two relatively small stages that are fairly close together, there isn't much room to install elaborate structures. The stages are pretty basic with minimal visuals, lasers, and pyrotechnics. Overall, production at this festival can definitely be improved upon.

D - Below Average
VARIETY

This festival is well balanced with a mixture of different genres of electronic dance music, as well as some rap/hip-hop mixed in. As with any festival, there are headliners for each day, at each stage. Attendees have the ability to quickly move from one stage to the other to choose which artist they prefer to see more at any given time. Options are limited, but at most times of the day, the artists at each stage will be playing somewhat different genres of music.

B - Above Average
ATMOSPHERE

The grounds for this festival leave a lot to be desired. A very baren layout with limited seating areas and only one art installation, as well as a lack of additions such as lighting or displays in the common areas is very disappointing. Knowing what Insomniac is capable of producing, I think they can do far better with minimal effort. Don't get me wrong, you can still have a great time at this event, with great music, people, etc. It's important to realize though, that standards going in should be lowered. All things stated, paired with the unimpressive stage production, this festival should be offered at a cheaper price.

D - Below Average
RETURN?

This is a fun festival to attend if you happen to live in the Bay Area and are looking for a local event without all the bells and whistles. I don't think it's worth traveling for though. You can do better with the cost of this ticket. Saving up for another festival that will provide a far better experience would be ideal. Going to this festival once and knowing how other festivals are makes my decision to save my money on this one quite easy.

No
