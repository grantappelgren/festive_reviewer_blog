ESCAPE

  • Location: San Bernardino, CA
  •  
  • Date: October
  •  
  • Duration: 2 Days
  •  
  • Time of Day: Day + Night
  •  
  • Stages: 4
  •  
  • Genre: Various
  •  
  • Host: Insomniac
PRODUCTION

Stage production at this festival is great. Each stage really sets a Halloween-like mood with set-ups like a grimm that extends forward (pictured above), a smiling clown with razor sharp teeth (pictured below), and more. Visuals, lasers, pyrotechnics, steam, and other effects supplement the mega-structures and make for an excellent stage experience. Additionally, confetti is often dropped or shot out over the crowd to further exemplify the feeling of being at a party.

A - Exceptional
VARIETY

Escape offers a large lineup with artists from almost every electronic dance genre. There are several options to choose from at any given time, and the festival tends to attract some pretty big names in the industry. Each stage doesn't necessarily feature a specific type of music, rather the larger stages typically house the more prominent artists, while the smaller stages will showcase more of the up-and-comers.

B - Above Average
ATMOSPHERE

Phenomenal. Insomniac really commits to creating the best Halloween party possible. From the amazing stages to the numerous art installations and acts that range from seasonal, to creepy, to downright scary, it is easy to tell that a tremendous amount of work was put into the event. Not to mention, many of the festival-goers (including the artists!) dress up in Halloween costumes to further add to the party experience. The ability to have a blast at a music festival without necessarily needing to even go to a stage and listen to music should explain how neat the environment is. Most definitely deserves an A.

A - Exceptional
RETURN?

Halloween is probably my favorite holiday, so I'm maybe being a bit biased. But this is really a must if you're at all into the holiday and electronic dance music. People go all-out on their costumes and Insomniac takes it's production to the extreme. The lineup is always great as well. This may not be an event that I attend every single year, but definitely one I'll try catch often. Highly recommend!
