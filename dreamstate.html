DREAMSTATE

  • Location: San Bernardino, CA
  •  
  • Date: November
  •  
  • Duration: 2 Days
  •  
  • Time of Day: Night
  •  
  • Stages: 3
  •  
  • Genre: Trance
  •  
  • Host: Insomniac
PRODUCTION

Stages are created to transport festival-goers into a cosmic world. This includes stages such as a disc-shaped one that's reminiscent of a UFO (pictured above), as well as an other-worldly city structure stage (pictured below) making you feel as if you are visiting another dimension. With visuals, lasers, pyrotechnics, and more rivaling the majority of other festivals, Insomniac exceeds exectations.

A - Exceptional
VARIETY

Dreamstate's slogan is "Your Trance Destination". That said, it's a festival featuring trance in its numerous forms (melodic, psychedelic, progressive, hard, etc.) Many renowned trance DJs from around the globe travel to perform here. Smaller-named DJs also gain exposure at this event. As one of the premier Trance festivals in the world, DJs know the crowd is made up of people who love this genre of music. Unfortunately, if you don't enjoy trance and aren't looking to see if you do, I wouldn't recommend this festival. However, for trance lovers, this is about as good as it gets!

D - Below Average
ATMOSPHERE

Like the festival's production, the atmosphere walking around the grounds gives a space-like vibe. Bright white, blue, and purpe lights luminate the common areas between stages, emmitting a calming feeling in an environment filled with commotion. In terms of age, the crowd is skewed towards a bit of an older demographic compared to other electronic dance music festivals. With that, comes a more "laid-back" feeling that you'd expect with that population. Regarding weather, it's fittingly cold, like it'd be in space! Don't let the location fool you, as Southern California can still get to temperatures in the 30's.

B - Above Average
RETURN?

Trance is my favorite genre, so Dreamstate is definitely a festival I really enjoy. The production is excellent and many of the artists who perform there are hard to catch at other festivals in California. There really isn't another trance festival offered in the US at this scale. As a trance fan, this is an event I plan to attend every year, and one I highly recommend to anybody interested in the genre.
