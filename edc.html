ELECTRIC DAISY CARNIVAL

  • Location: Las Vegas, NV
  •  
  • Date: May
  •  
  • Duration: 3 Days
  •  
  • Time of Day: Day + Night
  •  
  • Stages: 9
  •  
  • Genre: Various
  •  
  • Host: Insomniac
PRODUCTION

This is Insomniac's flagship festival. Realizing people travel from all over the world to attend the event, the company brings out its entire arsenal of lights, lasers, visuals, mega-stage structures, pyrotechnics, and more. With stages spanning several hundred yards to those more intimate and of a smaller scale, no aspect is skimped on. Volume is louder, and the stakes are heightened as attendees often have this event marked as the premier festival of the year that they look forward to. A nightly fireworks display is just one of the many showstoppers offered.

A - Exceptional
VARIETY

EDC offers basically every type of electronic dance genre you can imagine. From house, trance, techno, and trap, to everything in between, there's truly something for everybody. Some stages focus on featuring a certain genre, while others are more balanced and show sets from a plethora of different types of genres. This gives people the option to roam around and explore new forms of music all in one place, which is difficult to find with other festivals.

A - Exceptional
ATMOSPHERE

The Las Vegas Motor Speedway, where EDC is held is absolutely massive. Utilizing the entire infield and a portion of the bleachers, Insomniac creates an experience like none other. There are numerous art installations, some of which are interactive. Additionally, there are carnival characters and performers wandering around the grounds providing endless entertainment. Not to mention, there are carnival rides as well if you'd like a break from the music. I enjoy walking through the entire grounds just to see the different elements Insomniac incorporates. Another aspect of this festival that shouldn't go unnoticed is the ability to sit high-up on the speedway bleachers to relax and take in a gorgeous view of the entire festival. All in all, Insomniac knocks this one out-of-the-park.

A - Exceptional
RETURN?

This festival holds a special place in my heart as it was the first electronic dance music event I ever attended. To this day, it's ranked #1 on my list of best festival experiences. Not only do I enjoy the event itself, but the fact that it's in Las Vegas makes it a great vacation where I can stay at a hotel on the strip and have a blast during off-festival hours as well. I highly recommend this festival to anybody wanting an experience that words can't really describe. It's a trip that I have circled on my calendar every year.
