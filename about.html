ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Grant, the creator of Festive Reviewer! Growing up, I not only loved listening to music, but playing it as well. My parents would play a variety of classic and modern songs on the radio, and I played the piano and trumpet, both individually, and in bands. Together, this really made me appreciate the creativity and work of musicians. Surprisingly though, I never attended a music festival or any live events for that matter.

That all changed in May 2018. I finally decided to take a trip to EDC Las Vegas. To say I had a blast would be an understatement. The music was great, but the overall experience was like nothing I could've imagined. The energy and show production, along with the amazing sights and sounds made for an event that I'll never forget.

EDC Las Vegas features electronic dance music, and really introduced me to many of its subgenres. Realizing how much I enjoyed the music and atmosphere, I began attending other festivals in my home-state of California. Soon enough, it seemed like I was at just about every major electronic dance music festival the state had to offer.

Fast-forward to today, and I thought it'd be fun to both reflect and share my take on each of these festivals! Thanks for stopping by, I hope you enjoy the blog! Please feel free to contact me via Email or Instagram if you'd simply like to connect, or have any questions.
